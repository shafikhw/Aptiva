### Input schema for Advanced Apartments.com Scraper

#### `search_url` (string, optional)  
Search url to extract results from. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}  

---

#### `max_pages` (integer, optional)  
Maximum page to scrape. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}  

- `0` = no limit  
- Default value: `1`  

---

#### `filter_option` (enum, optional)  
Bedrooms filter option. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}  

Select your mode of navigation to go from point A to point B.  

**Allowed values:** :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}  
- `"all"`
- `"bed0"`
- `"bed1"`
- `"bed2"`
- `"bed3"`
- `"bed4"`

Default value: `"all"`  

---

#### `direct_urls` (array, optional)  
List of apartments urls. Extract info about a specific list of profiles. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}  

Example:  
- `https://www.apartments.com/found-study-chelsea-student-intern-new-york-ny/xe61mmq/`
